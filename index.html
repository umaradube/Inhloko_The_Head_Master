Ngathi

A Durban Staple, Rooted in Tradition

Inhloko The Head Master is more than a meat supplier — it is a Durban institution. We source directly from the abattoir, ensuring every cut is as fresh as it gets, delivered with the warmth and pride of KwaZulu-Natal culture.

From the iconic inhloko (ox head) to wholesale eggs, we serve households, spazas, caterers, and community events across greater Durban. Hygiene is never compromised. Tradition is always honoured.

Ihlanzeke — Hygiene First All products handled under strict hygiene standards from slaughter to your door.
Isiko — Respect for Culture We stock the cuts that matter in Zulu and Durban township cuisine. Culture is never an afterthought.
Ngqo Emsombulweni — Direct from Source No middlemen. Abattoir-fresh products mean better quality and fairer prices for you.
